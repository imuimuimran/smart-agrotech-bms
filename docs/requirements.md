# Smart AgroTech Business Management System (BMS)

## 1. Business Overview

### Project Name

Smart AgroTech Business Management System (BMS)

### Purpose

The Smart AgroTech Business Management System (BMS) is a web-based application designed to streamline and automate the daily operations of an agricultural machinery business.

The system will help manage:

- Products
- Categories
- Inventory
- Suppliers
- Purchases
- Customers
- Sales
- Customer Due Management
- Supplier Due Management
- Expenses
- Financial Reports
- Activity Logs
- Notifications

The application will be fully responsive and optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

---

## 2. Project Goals

The primary objectives of this system are:

- Digitize all business operations
- Reduce manual record keeping
- Improve inventory accuracy
- Track customer dues
- Track supplier dues
- Generate invoices
- Produce business reports
- Improve accountability using activity logs
- Support future business expansion

---

## 3. Target Users

### Admin

The Admin has full access to the system.

Responsibilities include:

- Manage users
- Manage products
- Manage categories
- Manage suppliers
- Manage customers
- Manage purchases
- Manage sales
- Record customer payments
- Record supplier payments
- Manage expenses
- View reports
- Configure system settings
- View activity logs

---

### Moderator

Moderators have limited operational access.

Responsibilities include:

- Manage products
- Manage customers
- Create sales
- Record customer payments
- View inventory
- View reports

Restrictions:

- Cannot manage users
- Cannot delete critical records
- Cannot access system settings
- Cannot change user roles

---

## 4. Supported Devices

## 6. Business Processes

### Product Purchase Process

Supplier delivers products

↓

Admin or Moderator creates a purchase

↓

Inventory increases automatically

↓

Supplier due is calculated

↓

Purchase record is stored

↓

Inventory log is created

---

### Product Sales Process

Customer selects products

↓

Admin or Moderator creates a sale

↓

Inventory decreases automatically

↓

Invoice is generated

↓

Customer due is calculated

↓

Sale record is saved

↓

Inventory log is created

---

### Customer Due Collection

Customer makes a payment

↓

Payment is recorded

↓

Remaining due is updated

↓

Payment history is saved

↓

Comment can be added

---

### Supplier Payment Process

Admin records supplier payment

↓

Supplier due decreases

↓

Payment history is stored

↓

Comment is saved
